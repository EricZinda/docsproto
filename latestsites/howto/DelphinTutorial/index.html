    At the 2013 DELPH-IN Summit, the first day was opened by a short ‘tutorial’ of key DELPH-IN ingredients—aiming to give a high-level picture of the DELPH-IN ecosystem and introduce relevant jargon for novices. The 2013 tutorial was presented by EmilyBender and StephanOepen.
